Key Overview

Vishal Hospital, led by Dr. A L Sinha, is a private hospital primarily specializing in General Surgery. Established in 2006, it has built a reputation for accessible and quality care in Rajkot. Its strategic location and long history underscore its commitment to serving a broad patient demographic from the city and surrounding areas.

Medical Services & Specialties

    Accreditations

    The hospital emphasizes high-quality care and patient satisfaction. However, formal accreditations like NABH or NABL are not explicitly mentioned in publicly available information for this specific facility. This contrasts with some other Rajkot hospitals that highlight such certifications.

    Absence of these doesn't mean substandard quality, but they are recognized external benchmarks.

    Insurance & Payment

    Information on accepted insurance policies is limited. The hospital is not prominently listed in cashless directories, suggesting a primary direct payment model. Patients may need to pay upfront and seek reimbursement.

    Direct inquiry with the hospital and insurer is highly recommended.

    Community & Recommendations

    Community Engagement

    Vishal Hospital states a "Community-Centric Approach," aiming to organize health camps and awareness programs. While this intent is positive, specific, publicly documented examples of these initiatives by *this* hospital are limited in the analyzed data.

    Key Recommendations for Patients

    1. Verify Insurance: Always confirm coverage with the hospital and your insurer.
    2. Confirm Hours: Call ahead for current schedules, especially for Dr. Sinha.
    3. Inquire on Specifics: For complex conditions, ask about specialized doctor availability.
    4. Use Feedback Wisely: Consider Justdial reviews, be aware of varied online presence.
    5. Direct Communication: For details on accreditations or services, contact the hospital.
