Key Overview

Vishal Hospital, led by Dr. A L Sinha, is a private hospital primarily specializing in General Surgery. Established in 2006, it has built a reputation for accessible and quality care in Rajkot. Its strategic location and long history underscore its commitment to serving a broad patient demographic from the city and surrounding areas.
